About Us

Hello, I'm Donna Hudson. I specialize in training Labrador Retrievers for bird hunting. With years of experience and a passion for the outdoors, I ensure that each dog reaches its full potential.

Training Programs

We offer a variety of training programs tailored to the needs of both beginners and experienced hunters. Our programs include basic obedience, advanced retrieval techniques, and specialized bird hunting skills.

Where We Train

Our training sessions are conducted in diverse outdoor environments to simulate real hunting conditions. From fields and forests to lakes and rivers, we cover all terrains to prepare your retriever for any situation.
